👦 Meet Zak

Hi, I'm Zak! 👋

My name is Zak, and I'm 10 years old. I love learning new things every day! Some of my favourite things are playing Minecraft, learning chess, reading about American and Medieval history, and learning how to do computer coding.

I built this games website as a fun project with my dad and my 5-year-old brother Zayd. 🧑‍💻 We want to make great free games that everyone can enjoy — with no ads and no trackers. Ever!

If you enjoy the games, please think about making a small donation to help us pay for the website. It means a lot to us! 💛
